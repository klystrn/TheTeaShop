    Earl Grey


    Known to improve digestion, Earl Grey is a classic tea blend which has been flavoured with the addition of oil of bergamot. The rind's fragrant oil is added to black tea to give Earl Grey its unique taste.

    English Breakfast

    Matcha


    Matcha is a powdered green tea produced from the ground, dried green tea leaves, making it rich in antioxidants. Our matcha tea has a unique, non-bitter taste that combines with our pure vanilla bean extract to produce a milky, nutty taste.
